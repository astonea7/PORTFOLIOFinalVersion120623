Music

I think my connection to music has been there from the start. I started playing instruments at a young age. Going from the recorder in 3rd grade, to the clarinet in 4th grade, and then onto the alto saxophone in 5th grade, I enjoyed getting to know more and more instruments. I stuck with the saxophone for 6 years and only stopped playing due to time constraints brought on by school. If I was able to afford a saxophone of my own and private lessons, I would definitely resume playing. During my years as a budding musician, I had the honor of getting to interact and play with many amazing people. I worked with renowned musicians such as Virigina Mayhew, Oscar Perez, Kelly Green, Bruce Williams, and more. I performed for the Nikil Badlani Foundation annually and also took part in concerts put on by Jazz House Kids. Although I stopped playing in bands around 2020, that did not dim my love for music. I registered up for my first Spotify account during 8th grade in 2016 and I have been a loyal user ever since. Music is apart of my daily life and I want to keep it that way as I search for a career.

Editing

Something I have always been intrigued by is video editing. Being able to use clips from movies, shows, and other media forms and pairing it together with the right audio to bring a new representation to life is something I admired. I wanted the ability to make my imagination a reality and editing seemed to be a way to do just that. I mostly found edits I liked to watch on social media, and this is where I reached out to an editor I loved and became their apprentice. With the Sony Vegas Pro 14 software they recommended and excitement to begin my journey, I taught myself how to make the edits I loved to watch. It was very challenging in the beginning and I gave up trying at times, but I came back to the program and kept on playing around with the features until things clicked. Once that occurred, I went from making slideshows with music on my phone to full blown official software edits which was a feat I only dreamed of reaching. Since anime is a genre of media I thoroughly enjoy, I made multiple anime edits and posted them on my Youtube channel which I am very proud of!

Social Media

I have been using social media since at least middle school, if not sooner. Being Generation Z, It has been a part of my life and I definitely embraced it. As mentioned before, I really love anime and sharing my interests with others, especially like minded individuals. Around 2015 or 2016, I created an Instagram account in order to have a place to post about a show I really liked at the time. It was never something I took seriously, more just a passion project to pass the time and find people who also enjoyed the show as much as me. In 2018, I began consistently posting content about the show and the account quickly gained traction in the community. By the end of 2019, I had received over 150,000 likes on the account and at its peak, I had over 3,000 followers. The story and production of the show dropped significantly in quality which led me to no longer being a fan and ultimately not keeping up with the account anymore, however; for the time that I did have and actively used it, I hit major milestones and learned social media skills I could possibly use in a career setting.
